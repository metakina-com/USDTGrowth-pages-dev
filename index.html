How to Use USDTGrowth

  1. Set Up Your Wallet:
    • Install [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/) or any compatible Ethereum wallet.
    • Create a new wallet and securely store your seed phrase.
    • Ensure your wallet has sufficient USDT and a small amount of ETH/BNB/TRX for gas fees.
  2. Connect Your Wallet:
    • Click the "Connect Wallet" button in the top navigation.
    • Select your wallet provider (e.g., MetaMask) and authorize the connection.
  3. Deposit USDT:
    • Navigate to the "Deposit USDT" section and click the "Deposit" button.
    • Enter the amount of USDT you wish to deposit.
    • Approve the transaction in your wallet to allow the contract to transfer your USDT.
    • Confirm the transaction and wait for it to be mined.
  4. Withdraw Interest:
    • Go to the "Withdraw Interest" section and click the "Withdraw Interest" button.
    • Confirm the transaction in your wallet.
    • Your accumulated interest will be transferred to your wallet.
  5. Withdraw Principal:
    • Navigate to the "Withdraw Principal" section and click the "Withdraw Principal" button.
    • Enter the amount of principal you wish to withdraw.
    • Confirm the transaction in your wallet.
    • The specified amount will be transferred back to your wallet.
  6. Withdraw All Funds:
    • Go to the "Withdraw All" section and click the "Withdraw All" button.
    • Confirm the transaction in your wallet.
    • All your principal and accumulated interest will be transferred to your wallet.
